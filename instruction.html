SNAKE 2D
Instruction
Movement

Use arrow keys to move the snake around the map.

Rules

Gain points by passing through single points on the map, called "balls". To win, collect as many as you can.

Known bugs

Beware of rapid moves in one axis, because that can reset your points! There is no database, no cookies, so you play just for your own pleasure.
